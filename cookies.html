What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They can be used for essential site functionality and to measure usage.

Cookies we use

Necessary cookies (always on)

  • Consent cookie: stores your cookie preferences so we don’t ask on every visit.

Analytics cookies (optional)

If enabled, we may load an analytics script (for example Google Analytics) to understand how the site is used. This only loads after you consent.

Manage your preferences

You can update your cookie preferences any time from the homepage footer (“Cookie preferences”). You can also clear cookies in your browser settings.
